HOW TO BUY
CREATE WALLET

Install Vespr Wallet or Eternl from the official website, create a new wallet and secure the seed phrase, then transfer ADA to your ADA address to buy a token on Cardano Chain.

CONNECT TO DEX

Connect to a DEX like dexhunter.io or splash.trade. Open the DEX website, click 'Connect Wallet', and select Eternl. Approve in Eternl to trade.

Swap ADA for Tokens

Select ADA in the 'from' field and your desired token in the 'to' field. Enter the amount, click 'Swap' or 'Exchange,' and confirm in Eternl.

Manage Your Tokens

Check your transaction status in Eternl under 'Activity' or on Cardanoscan. If the token isn't visible, click 'Assets,', 'Add Token', and enter the contract address.
